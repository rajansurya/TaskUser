How to use Assigner

Tasks Creation

1. Create a task and assign it to the right owner.

2. Task may be defined as deadline with specific date n time.

a . The deadline date appears in red colour besides the task summary

b. On the deadline date notification reminders come for deadline task

3. Assignee of the task is selected from the contact list and can be group also

a . Selected contact should have mobile number to receive the task
(contacts with only email id shall not receive it, as it is a mobile number based task management solution).

b . In case of group multiple tasks are created with owners as the member of group. Use this feature to allocate tasks to your complete team which is saved in the group option of phone.

4. Task can have urgency level as Low/Medium/High/Fire

a. If assignee does not have the application to receive task via application then shall receive it via SMS which is sent from assigner’s mobile only. Charges for sending sms will be as per your plan.

Task summarization

1. Tasks shall of three categories-(filter these out by using filter options on top)

a . Self- created by you for you

b . Received- created by someone else for you e.g. your boss or your team members

c. Assigned- created by you for someone else e.g. task assigned by you to your team members

2. Appears in chronological sequence.
Latest (new/updated) appears on top
Sync of tasks happen in 30 seconds but on demand can be done via Menu option.

a. Can view contact wise task summary for received as well as assigned tasks.

b. Can sort tasks on basis of Name/Priority/ Date (default)

c. Task received from a contact number which is not in contact list of user will go in junk tasks. Concept is that if you do not has some one’s number in your contact list then you do not expect a task from that person also. This way cluttering of tasks summary is protected.

3. Closed tasks can be viewed by clicking “close” state in menu options.

4. Tasks can be filtered out on following options from the top bar, which could be combination of these:

a . Self tasks

b . Received tasks

c . Assigned tasks

d . Tasks with urgency level as fire

e . Closely watched tasks


Task Management

1. Can assign urgency of task as fire by clicking fire icon and assignee will see fire against the task.

a . Fire tasks can be filtered out by clicking icon on top.

2. Use closely watched icon to focus on tasks which you want to focus upon. Closely watched is for self control only and is not visible to assignee. This is useful when you have too many tasks to manage.

a . Closely watched can be filtered out by clicking icon on top.

3. Can communicate with assignor/assignee using messaging option.

a . This way can pass on message/clarification/reminder

b . Can view summary of exchanged messages

c . By sending a message an old task can be pushed on top in assignee’s summary

d . Goes as notification also

4. Can close a task by clicking check box

a . Closed tasks can be viewed by clicking Close state in menu option

b . Tasks once clicked for closing appears strike off for 10 sec and then it moves to closed section

c . Task closed by assignee, appears as strike off in open list to assignor for 24 hrs after closing but can be moved to close list by clicking “close” action

5. Following actions are also possible on task:

a . Edit

b . Change scheduled/Deadline date

c. Change assignee of task
